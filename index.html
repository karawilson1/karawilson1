Hello! I am a digital product designer,
 visual designer,
 and former 5th grade teacher based in New York.
I am currently pursuing my master's degree in communication design at Parsons.

I created this space to make room for a less-stuffy, more-comprehensive view of who I am and what I value; if you'd like to see my design work, there's a button over there in the top right corner that will take you there. But if you'd like to stick around and hang out, that works too!

A couple things:

I used to be a 5th grade teacher. I left teaching in 2019 because I realized that my strengths were not oriented for direct instruction. How I understand the world is not tethered to anything physical; my worldview, which is constantly shifting, comes to me in the space between words, the precision of the language used around me, and how ideas are transmitted from one person to another. I like professionally exploring my worldview through design because the design process feels similar to how my brain works: concentric and iterative.

Professionally, I feel most energized when exploring the nonlinear and expansive parts of a project and digging deeper into the edgy details; I love creating things that energize people to explore the world at the ground level, meaning, I like when people can see their own ideas, habits, and interests in what I create.

I’m a big fan of the different examples of information design
 coming out of the editorial world that make people think about and explore subjects further. I also enjoy opportunities to infuse my work with a playful and intentional voice that makes the user feel personally seen by and engaged with it, because we need more of that on the world wide web!If you're interested or hiring, I would love to find a role
 that would allow me to engage with the multiplicities of UX/UI design through words- and ideas-centric digital products.
