Hello —

I am a digital product designer, visual designer, and former 5th grade teacher in New York City. I have a Master's degree in communication design from Parsons.

I love creating things that energize people to explore the world at the ground level, especially if that exploration leads to learning something new or shifting an existing worldview. I feel most energized thinking about how to design for words, stories, and ideas and what that means for the current moment and medium.
